Sean Rider


Music and Event Photographer from Fargo, North Dakota (now livng in St. Louis)



        Live music has been an obsession of mine ever since I saw Green Day in the Fargodome at the age of 16. Around the same time I was taking photography classes in high school and became obsessed with the concept of capturing the energy and emotion of concerts through images. Ever since I have been doing my best to capture the electric moments that any music goer craves.

        I have been practicing photography since 2009 and have been using photoshop since I was a kid. Feel free to contact me with any of your visual needs! If I can't find a way to make your creative vision a reality, I will do whatever I can to help you find someone who can.

Services include (but not limited to):

        - Live show photographs

        - Album cover and promo photos and design

        - Music videos and promo videos

        - Logo and merch design

        - Personal or group portrait sessions

        - Website design and creation
